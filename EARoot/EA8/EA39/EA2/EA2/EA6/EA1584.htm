Including job creation in your response to the "value for money and to the taxpayer" question is both appropriate and strategically powerful.<br/><br/>It shows that public funds are not only delivering a one-off innovation but stimulating wider economic impact, supporting the government’s Levelling Up, Net Zero, and SME growth agendas.<br/><br/><b><u>Job Creation and Long-Term Economic Return </u></b><br/>Beyond the immediate technical deliverables, the project represents strong long-term value to the UK taxpayer by catalysing job creation and regional economic growth. The commercial rollout of GreenTowerAI‑RuralConnect is projected to create 8–13 skilled full-time jobs within the lead organisation, spanning AI engineering, field deployment, software support, and commercial operations. An additional 10–15 roles are expected to emerge across UK-based suppliers supporting solar, telecom, and logistics services. These are sustainable, green economy roles aligned with the UK's future skills agenda. As such, the initial public investment not only unlocks near-term innovation but seeds a scalable, revenue-generating product line that will return long-term dividends to the UK economy through employment, tax contributions, and infrastructure self-reliance<br/><b><u><br/></u></b><br/>
