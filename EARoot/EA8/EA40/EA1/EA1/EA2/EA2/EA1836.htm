Hopefully you’ll never get to use your presentation. Your goal is to get the customers to talk, not you. This is the biggest idea in Customer Development. The real goal of the problem presentation is for you to capture your hypotheses about what you believe, but unlike an existing company, you aren’t trying to convince anyone you’re right. You’re there to listen.<br/>
