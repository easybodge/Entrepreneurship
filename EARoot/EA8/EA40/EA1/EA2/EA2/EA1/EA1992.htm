One of the mistakes startups often make is assuming their channel partners invest in creating customer demand. Most do not. For example, in Figure 9.12, don’t assume the book wholesaler does anything other than stock and ship books. The same is true for the distributor. It takes orders from bookstores and in some cases may promote book sales to bookstores, but it doesn’t bring customers into the store to buy. (Unfortunately nowadays most publishers don’t create demand either.)<br/><br/>
