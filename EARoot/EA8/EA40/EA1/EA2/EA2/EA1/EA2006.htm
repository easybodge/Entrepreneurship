Customer validation attempts to “test sell” at every stage. It runs a continuing series of quantitative pass/fail tests to determine whether there’s strong enough product/market fit to justify scaling sales and marketing spending.<br/><br/>PHASE 1 OF CUSTOMER VALIDATION PREPARES the tools to test the company’s ability to acquire customers. In this phase, you’ll craft your product positioning, which guides how you write the marketing, and online materials needed for the sales effort. For companies in the physical channel, other steps in this phase include development of collateral materials, channel sales plans, and a sales roadmap. Sometimes, a “sales closer” is also hired at this point.<br/><br/>Web/mobile channel startups develop their plans and tools for customer acquisition and activation as well as a dashboard or toolset for monitoring the results. They create a hi-fidelity MVP to be sure the acquisition efforts are valid.<br/><br/>
