VC Fred Wilson suggest some burn-rate guidelines for venture funded web/mobile businesses. So these figures are anticipated by VCs. <br/><br/>Customer Discovery phase which he calls 'building product', burn-rate between 50k to 75k /month, able to fund a team of three building MVP as well as rent, salaries, operating costs. <br/><br/>Customer Validation phase, 'building usage'. burn rate upto 100K a month. By that stage, product/market fit is achieved already and this stage fuel the 'get customers' and MVP iterations tasks. <br/><br/>Customer Creation, 'building the business' , hold burn-rate to 250K a month. Business here builds the team, expands marketing activities, starts generating material revenue. <br/>
