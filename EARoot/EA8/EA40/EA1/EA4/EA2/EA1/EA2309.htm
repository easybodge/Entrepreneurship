<ul>
<li>goal is to take as much market share from incumbent as possible.</li><li>focus is on demand creation and customer acquisition</li><li>estimate size of serviceable addressable market (based on what we can acquire considering our limitations, sales peoples, geography, resources etc. ) This is our first year sales target.</li><li>put together sales forecast based on knowledge from earlier stages (discovery, validation) and arrived pricing, knowledge of customers.</li><li>best way to arrive sales forecast is asking 1) if our products were free and there were no competitors, how many will we sell. 2) then if we had competitors, and product is free stil how many will we sell. 3) keep refining, given our pricing, how many will we sell.</li></ul>
Above points will give the sales revenue that startup can generate. <br/><br/>Then understand costs, for instance startup has to affort 'Product/Company Launch' costs and acqusition or formation of 'Distribution Channels'. Its worth utilizing the New Lancaster Strategy. i.e. 3 times the sales and marketing budget of competitor if its monopoly or at least 1.7 times if there are multiple competitors. <br/>
