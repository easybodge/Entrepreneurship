Bowling Alley<br/>Stage: Early Market Penetration<br/>Customers: Niche markets (early adopters, pragmatists in pain)<br/>Strategy: Land and expand in verticals<br/><br/>You're targeting specific vertical niches (like healthcare, education, or manufacturing) — each niche is a "bowling pin".<br/><br/>You solve a critical problem in one niche, build a strong use case, and then knock over the next pin by leveraging that success.<br/><br/>These niches are easier to sell to because they have urgent needs and are willing to try new solutions.<br/><br/>➡️ Goal: Build momentum and credibility in niche markets.<br/>
