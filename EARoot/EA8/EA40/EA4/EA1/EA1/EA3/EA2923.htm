The Executive Summary is a concise overview of your entire business plan. It should capture the essence of your business, outlining the problem, solution, market opportunity, competitive advantage, revenue model, growth strategy, financial highlights, and management team. This section is crucial because it’s often the first—and sometimes the only—section investors read<br/>
