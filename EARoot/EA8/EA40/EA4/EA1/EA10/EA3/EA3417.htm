Business Plan defines the genetic DNA of business that makes it unique. Why business exists? Why not others, competitors? Why these products and offerings within business and why not competitors? Why customers will buy products or offerings? Who are the customers? (target groups, segments, market etc). who are competitors? Overview of resources, team. Three financial plans (concervative, moderate, optimistic). showing realistic sales revenues, margins, expenses, profits preferably on monthly quarterly and annual basis. <br/><br/>must give metrics and KPIs<br/>
