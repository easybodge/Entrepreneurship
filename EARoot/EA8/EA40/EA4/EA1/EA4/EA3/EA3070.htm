These KPIs measure how well your business model is working in terms of revenue, profitability, customer acquisition, and overall sustainability.<br/><br/><b>Revenue & Growth Metrics</b><br/>Revenue Growth Rate (%) → Measures how fast your revenue is growing over time.<br/>Formula: (Current Revenue – Previous Revenue) / Previous Revenue × 100<br/><br/>Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) → The cost to acquire a new paying customer.<br/>Formula: Total Sales & Marketing Costs / Number of New Customers<br/><br/>Customer Lifetime Value (CLV/LTV) → Total revenue expected from a customer over their lifetime.<br/>Formula: Average Revenue per User (ARPU) × Customer Lifetime<br/><br/>Break-even Point (Months/Years) → Time taken to recover initial investment and become profitable.<br/>Formula: Fixed Costs / (Revenue per Unit – Variable Costs per Unit)<br/><br/><b>Operational Efficiency Metrics</b><br/>Gross Margin (%) → Measures profitability of products/services after direct costs.<br/>Formula: (Total Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold) / Total Revenue × 100<br/><br/>Burn Rate (Cash Outflow Rate) → Measures how quickly the company is spending capital.<br/>Formula: Total Expenses per Month<br/><br/>Recurring Revenue (%) → Indicates the percentage of revenue from subscriptions or repeat business.<br/>Formula: Recurring Revenue / Total Revenue × 100<br/><br/>Churn Rate (%) → Measures how many customers stop using the service over time.<br/>Formula: (Customers Lost / Total Customers) × 100<br/><br/><b>Market Penetration & Customer Growth</b><br/>Market Share (%) → Compares your company’s sales to total industry sales.<br/>Formula: (Your Revenue / Total Market Revenue) × 100<br/><br/>Sales Conversion Rate (%) → Measures how many leads turn into customers.<br/>Formula: (Total Customers / Total Leads) × 100<br/><br/>Customer Retention Rate (%) → Shows how well your company retains customers.<br/>Formula: (Customers at End of Period - New Customers) / Customers at Start of Period × 100<br/><br/>Net Promoter Score (NPS) → Measures customer satisfaction and likelihood of recommending the product.<br/>Scale: -100 to +100 based on customer survey responses.<br/><br/><b><u>Business Model Example</u></b><br/>Revenue Streams: Subscription model, direct product sales, licensing IP<br/>Customer Segments: Schools, colleges, STEM educators, private training institutions<br/>Channels: Online platform, direct sales, strategic educational partners<br/>Customer Relationships: Dedicated account managers, personalized online support, loyalty programs<br/>Key Resources: Educational content, online platform, AI-driven algorithms, technical expertise<br/>Cost Structure: Content creation, software development, staff salaries, marketing and sales<br/>
