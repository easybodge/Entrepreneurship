<font color="#cd0000"><b>Non-Participating Preferred Stock</b></font><br/><font color="#cd0000"><b>Definition:</b></font><br/><font color="#cd0000"><b>Non-participating preferred stock provides investors with a fixed dividend and a defined liquidation preference. In a liquidation or exit event, holders receive their predetermined amount (often a multiple of the original investment) before common shareholders receive any proceeds.</b></font><br/><font color="#cd0000"><b><br/></b></font><font color="#cd0000"><b>How It Works:</b></font><br/><font color="#cd0000"><b><br/></b></font><font color="#cd0000"><b>Liquidation Preference: Investors receive their investment back (often with a premium) up to the agreed amount.</b></font><br/><font color="#cd0000"><b>No Additional Participation: Once the preferred shareholders receive their specified amount, they do not participate further in any remaining proceeds. The rest goes solely to the common shareholders.</b></font><br/><font color="#cd0000"><b>Example:</b></font><br/><font color="#cd0000"><b>If a startup issues non-participating preferred shares with a 1x liquidation preference and later sells for a sum that, after paying the preferred shareholders their initial investment, leaves excess proceeds, those shareholders would not share in the extra amount; only common shareholders would receive that remainder.</b></font><br/><font color="#cd0000"><b><br/></b></font><font color="#cd0000"><b><br/></b></font><font color="#cd0000"><b><br/></b></font><font color="#cd0000"><b><br/></b></font><font color="#cd0000"><b>Participating/Non-Participating</b></font><br/><font color="#cd0000"><b>Preferred</b></font><br/><font color="#cd0000">• </font><b>Participating: </b>1X preference PLUS a pro-rata share<br/>of what remains.<br/><font color="#cd0000">• </font><b>Non-participating: </b>1X preference, no participation.<br/><font color="#cd0000">• </font>Preferred invest $2.5M on a $5M “post” valuation<br/>(50% ownership). Company sells for $10M.<br/><font color="#cd0000">– </font><b>Participating </b>– Preferred $6.25M; Common $3.75M<br/><font color="#cd0000">– </font><b>Non-Participating </b>– Preferred $5M; Common $5M<br/>
