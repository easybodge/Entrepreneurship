Appears In: do's and dont when meeting investors
<b><u>Don't</u></b><br/>Dont read slides<br/>don't talk about projections without a plan. have underlying projections, revenue, market size etc  in the appendix at least. <br/>Never lie and not exaggerate.<br/>Never say i have no competitors<br/>Focus only on positives. Emphasise on positives. <br/>Dont dwell on issues. <br/>Dont become argumentative or defensive. <br/>Dont obsess about financial stake. <br/><br/>Listen to anything they ask and answer. dont appear not listening<br/>dont answer question there is no answer. <br/><br/><b><u>Do's</u></b><br/>Connect emotionally with the investor <br/>Highlight prior success<br/>Emphasize team strength<br/>Demonstrate you understand the market<br/>discuss how you will gain competitive advantage<br/>show confidence that assumptions on revenue projects are very achievable and how i will achieve these. <br/>Show there is a plan for each major risk. <br/>Research you audience. <br/>if time allows, give product demo. <br/>Leave time for questions. <br/>Ask for adequate funding - that's why you're there. <br/>Less is more, be concise and to the point. <br/><br/>
