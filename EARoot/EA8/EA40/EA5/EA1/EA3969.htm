these are typical London Stock Exchange requirements. <br/>London Stock Exchange (Main Market – Premium/Standard listing)<br/><br/><ul>
<li>No strict revenue requirement.</li><li></li><li>Must have a market capitalisation ≥ £30M.</li><li></li><li>Must publish 3 years of audited accounts (with some exceptions).</li><li></li><li>25% of shares must be in public hands (free float).</li></ul>
<br/>For nasdaq<br/><br/><ul>
<li>Shareholders’ equity: at least $5M–$15M, depending on tier.</li><li>Public float: at least 1.25M shares held by the public.</li><li>Share price: minimum $4 per share (with some exceptions down to $2–3).</li><li>Market value / revenues:</li><li>Standard 1: pre-tax earnings ≥ $11M over the last 3 years, with at least $2.2M in each of two most recent years.</li><li>Standard 2: Market cap ≥ $50M, revenues ≥ $75M.</li><li>Standard 3: Market cap ≥ $160M (no revenue/earnings requirement).</li></ul><p/>
