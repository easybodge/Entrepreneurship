Here’s a straight‑copy list of “Signal‑style” platforms that let you search or match with investors by sector, stage, cheque size, geography, etc. — useful from the UK (and beyond):

OpenVC – https://openvc.app – Free, crowd‑curated database (+ built‑in mini‑CRM) with filters for sector, round size, geography, lead/will‑lead status and more. Also publishes niche lists like “AI investors Europe”.
OpenVC

Connectd – https://www.connectd.com – UK matching marketplace; upload your deck and an AI engine routes it to investors whose thesis matches your sector, stage and SEIS/EIS status (freemium).
connectd.com

UKBAA DealShare – https://ukbaa.org.uk (members area) – Private deal‑discovery board inside the UK Business Angels Association; 30‑plus sector filters, region and ticket‑size filters; free for members.
ukbaa.org.uk

SeedLegals → Find Investors / Pitch – https://seedlegals.com/raise/pitch – Directory of >4 000 angels & funds plus warm‑intro workflow when you run your round on SeedLegals; filter by sector, cheque size, SEIS/EIS.
SeedLegals

Angel Investment Network (UK) – https://www.angelinvestmentnetwork.co.uk – Post your deal or browse 290 000+ angels; 33 sector tags, ticket‑size and location filters; pay‑on‑success model.
Angel Investment Network

F6S Investor Directory – https://www.f6s.com/investment-funds – Global startup community directory with filters for industry, funding stage and country; free.
F6S

DocSend Fundraising Network – https://www.docsend.com/fundraising-network – Submit your deck; if accepted, it’s sent to 270 + actively investing VCs matched to your stage & sector; free.
docsend.com

Landscape & OpenScout – https://landscape.vc / https://openscout.landscape.vc – “Glassdoor for VCs” plus investor search/matching by sector, cheque size and founder review score; free.
landscape.vc
openscout.landscape.vc

Crunchbase Pro – https://pro.crunchbase.com – Advanced search with hundreds of filters (industry, stage, past round lead, geography, etc.); export contacts; paid subscription.
Crunchbase

Dealroom.co (Founder view) – https://dealroom.co – Investor screener covering 120 000 funds/angels; filter by industry, recent activity, ticket size and HQ city; free lite tier & premium plans.
Dealroom.co

Beauhurst – https://www.beauhurst.com – Deep UK‑only data (40 k funds & angels) with >300 filters including SIC codes, buzzwords, cheque size and SEIS status; paid.
Beauhurst

PitchBook Starter – https://pitchbook.com – Global private‑markets DB; investor screener with filters for vertical, round size, geography, past exits; paid.
PitchBook

Sifted Pro Investor Ranking – https://sifted.eu/rankings/uk-ireland-2025-leaderboard-investors – Interactive tables of the most active UK & European VCs; filter by industry focus, cheque count, HQ, gender balance; freemium.
Sifted

AngelList / Wellfound Capital – https://angel.co – Directory of syndicates and rolling funds; filter by market, stage, location, ticket size; free.
angel.co

OpenVC “VC‑List‑of‑Lists” – https://openvc.app (Resources > Lists) – Meta‑index linking to 50 + public VC spreadsheets & Airtables (e.g. “Climate pre‑seed Europe”); free.
OpenVC
